What a single fall sets in motion

It's never just a fall.

A fall doesn't end when the ambulance leaves. It triggers a cascade — physical, emotional, and financial — that reshapes entire families.

The Physical Cascade
A hip fracture means surgery. Surgery means hospitalization. Hospitalization means immobility. 25% of seniors who fracture a hip die within 12 months — not from the fall, but from everything it set in motion.
The Loss of Home
Most seniors who experience a serious fall never return to full mobility. Many leave the home they raised their family in — not because they wanted to, but because the fall made the decision for them.
The Family Impact
Average fall hospitalization: over $30,000. But the real cost is the guilt of not knowing sooner. The what-ifs that follow you. The fear every time the phone rings from an unknown number.
"By the time a fall happens, the window to prevent it has already closed."

The Technology

Five platforms. One mission.

Each Haven platform works independently. Together, they create the most comprehensive fall prediction system available to families.

Steadfast™

Passive In-Home Sensor Network

Small, plug-in radar sensors placed in the bedroom, bathroom, and kitchen. Steadfast detects movement, transitions, and presence without cameras. No compliance required — it simply works.

  • Radar-based — no cameras, no video, no microphones
  • Detects room transitions, movement speed, and presence
  • Zero compliance — plug in and forget
  • The always-on foundation of Haven's AI
📡

Stride™

Predictive Gait Intelligence Engine

The AI-powered engine that builds a personal gait fingerprint for each individual. Stride analyzes walking speed, cadence, stride length, and symmetry — detecting the subtle degradation patterns that precede a fall.

  • Builds dynamic personal baselines — not population averages
  • Detects degradation weeks before it's clinically visible
  • Integrates radar, wearable, and vision data streams
  • Haven's core competitive moat and IP asset
🧠

Vigil™

Camera-Enabled Computer Vision

Real-time gait and posture analysis via pose estimation AI. The most data-rich monitoring available — designed for highest-risk seniors and institutional deployments requiring maximum confidence.

  • Computer vision detects micro-changes in movement
  • Privacy tiered — families control the level
  • Serves both individual consumers and assisted living facilities
  • Captures subtleties other sensors miss
👁

Anchor™

Matter-Compatible Smart Home Hub

The nerve center of every Haven home. Anchor aggregates data from Steadfast sensors, Vigil cameras, and connected wearables — running edge AI inference locally before pushing unified insights to Beacon. Matter-compatible, so it integrates with Apple Home, Google Home, Amazon Alexa, and SmartThings.

  • Edge AI processing — data stays in the home first
  • Matter protocol — works with existing smart home devices
  • Triggers smart home automations based on wellness data
  • The bridge between Haven's sensors and the family dashboard
⚓

Beacon™

Family Dashboard & Alert Intelligence

The daily interface for families and caregivers. Beacon turns raw sensor data into risk scores, trend graphs, and plain-English health narratives you can actually understand.

  • Daily risk score on a continuous scale
  • Tiered alerts with AI-generated context
  • Weekly health narratives in plain English
  • Achievement system that celebrates progress
🔔
How It Works

Invisible protection.
Visible peace of mind.

Haven learns what "normal" looks like, then watches for the subtle changes that precede a fall — days or weeks before it happens.

1
Set Up in Minutes
Plug in small radar sensors around the home. Connect a wearable they already own. Complete a quick baseline assessment. No technician, no complexity.
2
Learn Their Normal
Over 14 days, Haven builds a personal behavioral baseline — gait patterns, sleep rhythms, movement habits. Not population averages — what's normal for them.
3
Predict the Drift
The AI detects subtle deviations — stride shortening, nighttime trips increasing, balance declining. Individually easy to miss. Together they tell the whole story.
4
Protect Before the Fall
Beacon alerts your family with context — not a panic alarm. "Mom's stride shortened 12% this week. Here's what that could mean and what you can do."
