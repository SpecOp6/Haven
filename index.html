The Problem

Falls are the #1 killer of older adults.
Current solutions only react.

By the time a fall happens, the window to prevent it has already closed. Every existing product works the same way: wait for someone to hit the ground, then send an alert. That's not prevention.

Today's solutions

  • Wearable panic buttons users forget or refuse to wear
  • Camera systems that invade privacy
  • Motion sensors that can't distinguish a fall from sitting down
  • React after the damage is done

The Haven approach

  • Passive plug-in sensors — nothing to wear, nothing to press
  • Works with the watch they already own
  • Detects gait deterioration weeks before a fall
  • Predicts and prevents — not just detects
