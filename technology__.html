STEADFAST™

Passive In-Home Sensor Network

The sensor array that sees without watching.

Steadfast is Haven’s plug-in sensor network — discreet devices placed in bedroom, bathroom, and kitchen that detect movement, transitions, and presence without cameras. No compliance required from the senior. Steadfast simply works.

The always-on foundation layer of the Haven ecosystem. Plug them in, and they start building a picture of how your loved one moves through their day.

Discreet plug-in sensors — no installation, no wiring, no cameras

Whole Home

Covers bedroom, bathroom, kitchen, and living areas

Zero Compliance

Nothing to wear, charge, or remember — Steadfast just works

Always On

24/7 passive monitoring of movement and presence patterns

STRIDE™

Wearable Gait Intelligence

The watch they already wear, now working harder.

Stride overlays existing wearables — Apple Watch, Fitbit, Garmin, and other popular devices — to build a continuous personal gait and movement baseline. It tracks step frequency, stride length, sleep patterns, and balance trends.

This is the signal layer that makes AI prediction possible. Stride turns the watch your parent already owns into a fall prevention tool — no new hardware required.

Gait Baseline

Monitors sleep quality, daily activity, and behavioral patterns

VIGIL™

Computer Vision Gait Analysis

Real-time risk scoring for maximum confidence.

Vigil is Haven’s optional computer vision platform for families requiring the highest level of coverage. It monitors gait and posture in real time and delivers a 3-tier risk score — Low, Watch, and Elevated — giving families and care teams actionable intelligence at a glance.

Designed for higher-risk seniors and institutional environments like assisted living and memory care. Privacy tiered and family controlled.

Gait Analysis

Real-time computer vision tracking of gait patterns and posture

3-Tier Risk Score

Low, Watch, and Elevated — contextual, never binary

DTC & Institutional

Serves both home and assisted living / memory care environments

Family Controlled

Privacy tiered — families decide the level of monitoring

ANCHOR™

Edge Computing Hub

Your data stays home. Period.

Anchor is the brain of the Haven ecosystem — an in-home edge computing hub that processes all sensor and wearable data locally. Raw data never leaves the home. Only derived, actionable insights are delivered to the Beacon dashboard.

Built on the Matter smart home standard, Anchor integrates with Apple Home, Google Home, Alexa, and SmartThings — fitting seamlessly into the connected home your family already uses.

Edge Processing

Matter Compatible

Privacy First

Always Connected

Delivers processed data to Beacon in real time

BEACON™

Family Dashboard & Alert Intelligence

Your mom had a great week — her balance score improved 8%.

Beacon is the daily interface for families and caregivers. Delivered via the Haven app or any web browser, Beacon shows risk scores, trend graphs, tiered alerts with AI context, achievement milestones, and weekly health narratives.

This is what you interact with every day. Not clinical charts — human insights about how your loved one is doing, delivered in language that makes sense.
