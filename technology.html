📡 Platform 1

Steadfast

The sensor array that sees without watching.

Steadfast is Haven's multi-modal RF sensing hardware — a passive sensor array that detects human movement patterns through radio frequency analysis. No cameras, no microphones, no wearables. Just invisible signals that map how someone moves through their home.

Ceiling-mounted sensors cover entire rooms, work in all lighting conditions, and penetrate steam and light barriers — critical for bathrooms where 80% of falls occur.

Wi-Fi CSI

IEEE 802.11bf whole-home presence and movement detection

mmWave Radar

60GHz FMCW for sub-centimeter gait analysis in high-risk zones

Edge Processing

On-device ML inference — raw data never leaves the home

Hardware BOM

🧠 Platform 2

Stride

The AI that learns how you walk.

Stride is Haven's analytics and machine learning engine. It builds a personalized gait profile for each individual, tracks changes over time, and detects the subtle anomalies that precede a fall — often weeks before it would occur.

Using longitudinal gait analysis, Stride transforms raw RF data into clinically actionable risk scores. Published studies show gait analysis achieves 90–95% sensitivity in predicting fall risk.

🔔 Platform 3

Beacon

Smart alerts, not alarm fatigue.

Beacon is Haven's intelligent notification and escalation engine. Unlike simple alert systems that cry wolf, Beacon uses context-aware tiering to send the right information to the right person at the right time.

Trend-level risk data goes to family members. Clinical anomalies go to care teams. Acute events trigger emergency response. No unnecessary panic, no missed warnings.

🏥 Platform 4

Vigil

Clinical intelligence, delivered.

Vigil is Haven's clinical provider dashboard — the bridge between in-home sensing data and clinical decision-making. Geriatricians, physical therapists, and care teams get longitudinal movement data that transforms fall prevention from a reactive conversation into a proactive care plan.

Vigil integrates with existing EHR systems and supports Medicare CPT code 99454 for remote physiologic monitoring reimbursement.

Sensing layers
Multi-modal RF architecture
Three complementary sensing layers provide redundant coverage from whole-home awareness to sub-centimeter precision.

Wi-Fi CSI (802.11bf)

Whole-home coverage

Uses existing home Wi-Fi infrastructure to detect presence, room-to-room movement, and gross activity patterns. Leverages Channel State Information from standard routers and IoT devices. Minimal additional hardware cost.

mmWave FMCW Radar

High-risk zone precision

60GHz frequency-modulated continuous wave radar provides sub-centimeter resolution for gait analysis, breathing rate, and real-time fall detection. Works through steam and darkness — critical for bathrooms.

Supplementary Sensors

Contextual intelligence

Optional passive RFID for medication tracking, pressure mats for bed exit detection, and environmental sensors (temperature, humidity, light) for behavioral context enrichment.
